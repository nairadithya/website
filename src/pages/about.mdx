About pages are hard. I originally shied away from writing too much about myself here, because there's so much more to these sentences than what I write here. It's hard to condense myself down into something that's readable in about 2 minutes. Here's my best attempt.

I live in Bengaluru, pursuing a bachelor's degree in AI & Data Science, hacking on whatever I find interesting. If you'd like to know more about what I'm working on, my [portfolio](https://nairadithya.github.io/) is your best bet.

I'm a huge film nerd, and I occasionally post reviews on [Letterboxd](https://letterboxd.com/nairadithya/). I enjoy thinking about why they work and why they move us so deeply. I love watching video essays especially [Every Frame A Painting](https://www.youtube.com/@everyframeapainting) and any of their contemporaries. I enjoy wondering why certain pieces of art work when others don't. I like deconstructing what made a film fail and thinking about what could have been done to fix them.

I'm an Emacs evangelist. It's a beautiful paradigm for computing, and the process of using it gives me a lot of joy. If you're a fellow Emacs enjoyer or if you're Emacs-curious, reach out! While you're at it, check out my [handrolled Emacs config](https://github.com/nairadithya/.emacs.d).

I enjoy playing games. I don't like multiplayer games but there's so much beauty and joy in single player experiences. My all time favourites include [Dishonored](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dishonored), [Hollow Knight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hollow_Knight), [Mirror's Edge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mirror's_Edge) and anything touched by Valve.

I'm one of the hosts of the [amrita.town](https://amrita.town) webring along with [Nikhil](https://heftymouse.me). Check out a [random site](https://amrita.town/random).

I also host [ATLAS](https://atlasdev.club/), a systems programming community within Bangalore, largely centered in Amrita BLR.
